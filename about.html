Our Story

PetStop Map was born from a simple frustration: the difficulty of finding truly pet-friendly places while traveling. After countless hours spent calling hotels, researching cafes, and reading through endless reviews, we knew there had to be a better way.

We created PetStop Map to be the comprehensive resource we wished existed – a platform where pet owners can easily discover, review, and share pet-friendly locations worldwide. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or a cross-country adventure, we're here to help you and your pet travel with confidence.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of pet lovers, helping them create unforgettable memories with their four-legged family members.
